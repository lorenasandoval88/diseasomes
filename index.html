PRS [code] [notebook] [discussion] [ tutorial]
Individual polygenic risk score calculation (PRS) for 23andme reports, using the PGS Catalog.
Privacy notice: All data and calculations take place in the browser. No data or calculations circulate by a server. This application is, foremost, an exercise in consumer-facing privacy-preserving biomedical informatics.

Limitations: This calculator was devised as a proof of concept and it has a number of important limitations for research or clinical use. First, the current tool uses pre-imputation genotype data and thus results in a low number of SNP matches. Second, the tool only provides the raw PRS and does not calculate relative or absolute risk of disease. Further considerations are also required prior to clinical use of PRS (Hao et al 2022).
